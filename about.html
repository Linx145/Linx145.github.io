About me

My name is Linus, better known as Linx145. I'm an 18 year old student currently studying at Singapore Polytechnic. I'm also the founder and director of Cloud of Pinky LLP, an indie game development company focused on telling stories through our games, pioneering new and more efficient technologies and making the world a better place one person at a time.

Currently working on our flagship product, Harpy Raiders, I draw upon my rich imaginations and surreal dreamscapes of which I remember vividly to provide me with inspiration that is out of this world. In Somnio Omnia Licent - In a dream, anything is possible, and we at Cloud of Pinky dare to dream.

Beyond my role as project lead, I am many things. A programmer with 9 years of experience, a pixel artist with 6 years of experience. A writer, a designer, an amateur musician. If there is a project that needs to be completed, you can count on me and my hyperfocus to get things done.
